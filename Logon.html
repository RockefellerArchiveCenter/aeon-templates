All requests for materials held at the Rockefeller Archive Center are made through RACcess, our request system.

With this system you can:

  • Submit requests for archival materials or library books at the RAC
  • Submit requests in advance of a visit, or while at the RAC
  • Submit requests for scans or photocopies of archival materials
  • Track the status of requests, and access detailed information about past requests
